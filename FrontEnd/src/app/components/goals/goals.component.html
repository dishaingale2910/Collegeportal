    Goal & Objectives Of College


    With the objective of promoting, spreading and advancing education in the region, the "CHME Society" was registered in 1935
    and the college was established in 1935 campus is renowned as “Rambhoomi”.

    To achieve this goal college is working with the following objectives:


  • 1. To impart quality based higher education.
  • 2. To promote analytical and empirical research particularly of the delicate problems related to the society residing in the nearby areas.
  • 3. To develop responsible, sensitive youths having social commitment.
  • 4. To broaden the vision so that the youth have a panoramic view of the society.
  • 5. To create human resources with modern views and foresight which can be used for the well being and development of the country.
