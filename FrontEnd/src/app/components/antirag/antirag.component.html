Anti-Ragging


It is brought to the notice of the students that ragging is a criminal offence and AICTE has framed regulation which has been notified vide F.No.37-3/Legal/AICTE/2009 dated July 1, 2009
(available on AICTE web portal aicte-india.org) on curbing the menace of ragging in AICTE approved Technical Institution , in order to prohibit , prevent and eliminate the scourge of
ragging in AICTE approved Technical Institutions. The above regulation is mandatory and applied to all students. DMI has constituted Anti-Ragging-Committee, Anti-Ragging
Squads and Professional Counselors AICTE Regulation. Any violation of AICTE Regulation as cited above by the student shall call for punitive action against him/her. Students in distress
owning to ragging related incidents can contact Anti-Ragging-Committee of the institute or Toll Free Helpline 1800-180-5522 or write to the Member Secretary, AICTE ,7th Floor,
Chanderlok Building,Janpath, New Delhi-110001.
