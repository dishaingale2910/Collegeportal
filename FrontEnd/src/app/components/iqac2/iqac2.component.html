Goals and Objective


    Goals and Objective:


  • 1. To achieve academic and administrative excellence Creating a student-centric atmosphere for holistic learning.
  • 2. To develop a system for the conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the over all performance of the institution.
  • 3. To promote Interdisciplinary and collaborative research.
  • 4. To enhance the quality of staff by promoting and encouraging overall faculty development.
  • 5. Making “Quality Enhancement” as an integral part of the institutional functioning - Internalization and Institutionalization of quality enhancement initiatives.
  • 6. To assure all the stakeholders the accountability of the institution for its own quality.
  • 7. To enhance Infrastructure for Teaching-Learning and Administration


    The IQAC Team Believes In Planning Based On:


  • 1. Analysis of current trends
  • 2. Current needs of the education field
  • 3. Continuous improvement in teaching-learning and evaluation
  • 4. Use of latest technology for the betterment of students
  • 5. Social affairs and their impact on education
  • 6. Institutional Social responsibility
  • 7. Appropriate decision-making
  • 8.Effective implementation of the plans.
