Earn & Learn Scheme:


Objective:


  • 1. To help needy and poor students.
  • 2. To create Knowledge servant.
  • 3. To develop work culture among students.


Eligibility:


  • 1. The Student must be regular to the institute.
  • 2. Income of student’s parents should be less than Rs. 2,00,000/-


Implementation of the Scheme:


  • 1. Office work such as – typing on a computer are assigned.
  • 2. Students are assign work not more than 3 Hrs.
  • 3. Rs. 25/hrs. Remuneration is given to the students.
  • 4. Attendance of students regularly maintained.
  • 5. Remuneration is paid through account paychecks.
